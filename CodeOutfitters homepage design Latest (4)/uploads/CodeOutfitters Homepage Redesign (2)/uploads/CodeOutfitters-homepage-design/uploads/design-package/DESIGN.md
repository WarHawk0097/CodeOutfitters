# DESIGN.md

## Page purpose
CodeOutfitters homepage for a premium AI automation / intelligent systems studio. The page sells business automation outcomes: AI agents, workflow orchestration, data systems, CRM alignment, analytics, and deployment support.

## Target audience
Founders, operations leaders, service business owners, SaaS operators, agencies, and SMB teams that need automation systems but want a polished, trustworthy vendor rather than a generic automation freelancer.

## Business/category
AI automation agency / AI systems studio / business workflow automation consultancy.

## Visual style
- Light-mode premium SaaS website.
- Warm ivory page background.
- Emerald/teal as the primary brand accent.
- Soft gold accents used sparingly on hero pedestal edges, icon accents, glows, stars, and decorative particles.
- Mixed editorial + product UI aesthetic: bold serif-like hero headline, clean sans-serif body and UI labels.
- 3D glass illustration in hero.
- Editable UI cards in feature section.
- Elegant dashboard-like widgets without looking like a default template.

## Emotional tone
Confident, calm, premium, trustworthy, intelligent, business-first, outcome-focused.

## Brand personality
Precise, modern, technical, helpful, polished, reliable, quietly premium.

## Page mood
Soft, bright, airy, elegant, with subtle automation/AI futurism.

## Design personality
The design is not a generic dark AI landing page. It is a warm light-premium automation homepage with strong editorial typography, soft glass 3D illustration, business dashboard proof cards, and a restrained teal/gold visual system.

## What makes the design non-generic
- Three-line hero headline with only the final outcome line in teal.
- Large right-side 3D automation visual with translucent panels and gold/teal highlights.
- Trust row placed immediately under hero with minimal grayscale brand marks.
- “What We Build” section uses six different mini-dashboard/service cards, not a same-size icon grid.
- Process timeline uses horizontal numbered nodes with a single connecting line.
- CTA section combines text, avatar cluster, stars, and stat strip in a soft rounded panel.

## Section-by-section design intent

### Navbar
Simple top navigation, logo left, centered nav links, primary CTA on the right. It should feel refined and uncluttered.

### Hero
Left side communicates the main business promise. Right side proves capability visually through a premium 3D automation platform. Buttons are clear: primary audit CTA and secondary process/education CTA.

### Trust strip
A restrained proof band. Brand logos are grayscale/dark neutral, evenly distributed, low emphasis.

### What We Build
Show practical service categories as business systems, not generic marketing cards. Cards include mini UI widgets: gauge, calendar, CRM flow, lead rows, analytics chart, radar shield.

### Process
A horizontal step-by-step delivery path. It communicates method and reliability.

### CTA
Final conversion area. Warm rounded container with strong headline, audit button, avatar trust cluster, star proof, and stat cards.

## Core visual motifs
- Emerald/teal accent strokes.
- Warm ivory surfaces.
- Thin gold highlights.
- Rounded rectangles.
- Soft inner shadows.
- Very light borders.
- Subtle dashboard widgets.
- Glass/translucent panels.
- Dotted radial pattern.
- Horizontal process line.
- Circular metrics and radar rings.

## Background style
Primary background is off-white / warm ivory with subtle edge warmth. Most sections sit on the same continuous background, separated by thin horizontal divider lines and card shadows.

## Image/illustration style
Hero illustration is a soft 3D isometric glassmorphism visual:
- layered ivory platform;
- gold edge strip;
- teal glow beam;
- translucent glass panels;
- minimalist service icons;
- small vertical decorative rods;
- soft particles;
- low-contrast background glow.

## Icon style
- Thin, rounded, teal line icons.
- Simple SVG-like geometry.
- Minimal filled accent areas.
- Service icons should remain editable SVG.
- Avoid emoji icons or heavy illustrative icon packs.

## Card style
Cards are large warm-white rounded rectangles with:
- light beige borders;
- soft shadow;
- 18–24px radius;
- internal dividers where needed;
- small dashboard widgets;
- varied internal layouts.

## Button style
Primary button:
- teal/emerald gradient or solid teal.
- white text.
- medium radius.
- subtle shadow.
- strong visual priority.

Secondary button:
- off-white/transparent fill.
- teal text.
- light border.
- small circular arrow icon inside.

## Typography mood
- Hero and section headlines feel editorial, high-contrast, elegant.
- Body/UI text is clean and modern sans-serif.
- Small labels are uppercase, letter-spaced, teal.

## Color mood
Warm neutral base + teal confidence + gold premium warmth.

## Glow/shadow style
- Shadows are soft and low-opacity.
- Glows are teal for system energy, gold for warmth.
- No harsh neon.
- No heavy dark sci-fi glow.

## CTA hierarchy
Primary CTA appears in navbar, hero, and final CTA. The audit button is the strongest action. Secondary CTA appears only in hero.

## Trust/proof style
Proof is subtle: logos, avatars, stars, stats, process steps. It should not look like fake hype.

## Do design rules
- Keep the final reference image as the only source of truth.
- Build components as editable layers.
- Use assets only for complex visuals and decorative elements.
- Keep whitespace generous.
- Preserve asymmetry and varied card layouts.
- Keep all UI copy editable.

## Don’t design rules
- Do not create a dark sci-fi homepage.
- Do not convert service cards into equal icon cards.
- Do not rasterize full cards/sections.
- Do not add new sections.
- Do not add random gradients, blobs, or floating shapes.
- Do not use generic stock dashboards.
- Do not change typography mood.
- Do not replace warm ivory with pure white.
