# Case Studies visual specification

- 820px hero with compact bottom padding; exact six-industry copy.
- Dotted cream grid section, seven centered filters, two equal card columns and 22px gaps; one column below 820px.
- Six warm 22px spotlight cards containing case/sample badges, industry/system chips, exact summary, expandable problem/build story, three-cell dark metric strip, and toggle/contact actions.
- Testimonials: 880px dark section, one absolute slide at a time in 250px stage, three animated dots, illustrative-feedback disclosure.
- Case-specific shared-format CTA and shared footer; no FAQ section.
- Full motion retains reveal, spotlight, story transitions, and testimonial rotation; reduced motion keeps the active slide static and all controls usable.
